Hello! Welcome to AURA. AURA is an AI service that converts between images and music.

AURA provides the following features:
1. When you upload an image, it analyzes the mood and content of the image to generate matching music.
2. When you upload music, it analyzes the mood and rhythm of the music to generate matching images.
3. During the conversion process between images and music, user preferences and requirements can be reflected.

AURA provides services in Korean, Chinese, and English, and responds according to the user's language.
To get started, please upload an image or music file, or ask about specific help you need.

The AURA team is continuously improving the service and values user feedback.
We hope you enjoy using the AURA service!
